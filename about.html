About Our Project

Our project, Battery-Friendly Charging Coach, aims to revolutionize electric vehicle (EV) charging with cutting-edge machine learning models that optimize charging processes and ensure longer battery life.

Project Goals

The main goal of our project is to offer a safer, more efficient, and eco-friendly solution for electric vehicle charging. By integrating machine learning, we optimize the EV charging process, detect abnormal conditions, and extend battery life, ensuring a more sustainable driving experience.

Key Features

  • Advanced charging models based on user data.
  • AI-powered anomaly detection for safe charging practices.
  • Enhanced battery lifespan with optimized charging cycles.
